New Features


Reorganizable Menus

You can now adjust the position of the new main page menus to move your most used features to the top for easy access. Menus can be collapsed and expanded to put only necessary content in view. Simply drag a menu to change it's position or double click on the top of the menu to minimize it.

 

Sliding Stats

Click on the Stats heading on the left side of your screen to see detailed information about your site. Click the heading again to hide the detail.

 

Sortability

You can now sort most tables. Try clicking on the name of a column to sort it by alphabetical or numerical order. This makes it a lot easier to find a specific item in a long list.

Multiple Looks

You can now change the look of your interface to many different styles. See Change Look and Feel for more information.
